Rising stars - teams

Teams improving fastest this season. Momentum = a team's game-win% in the recent half of its schedule minus its early half (split at the midpoint match day). Positive means they're surging late; negative means cooling off. Because rosters and team names change between seasons, team momentum is measured within the current season only - our data only has team rosters and the match schedule for the live season, so (unlike players) teams can't be tracked across seasons.
